José Meza
José Meza is an interaction designer at IDEO's Palo Alto studio. Leveraging his background in architecture and communication design, José believes design should operate at multiple scales and be informed by a cross-section of disciplines.
José holds a Masters degree in architecture from Princeton University and a Bachelors degree in Architecture from Yale University. Prior to joining IDEO, José helped launch an early stage start-up in the Bay Area that builds collaborative design software for UX/UI designers. José has interned at New York brand agencies, Pentagram and 2x4, and at the architectural firm, Ateliers Jean Nouvel, in Paris.
