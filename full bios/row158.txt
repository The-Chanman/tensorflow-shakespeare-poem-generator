Felicia Chiao
Felicia is an industrial designer in San Francisco's Design for Food studio. Her experience in toy design and licensing combined with a background in illustration and machine tools helps her tackle projects with a playful and unique twist. She has a passion for physical prototyping and enjoys creating tangible deliverables and experiences. Felicia has created products for a broad range of clients, including Taco Bell, KFC, and Ainsworth Pet Nutrition. Prior to joining IDEO, she worked with Little Tikes and Lego Education.
Felicia earned her BA in industrial design at the Rhode Island School of Design.
