Wenting Guo
Wenting is a Senior Interaction Designer at IDEO Cambridge Office. With a background in both Interaction Design and Industrial Design, she approaches projects with an artistic spirit and sensibility while incorporating her deep understanding of technology. She crafts experiences at different scales and through different mediums, ranging from digital interfaces, products, to larger interactive environments.
During her time at IDEO, Wenting has been involved in designing digital services and physical experiences for clients from a wide variety of industries, including automotive, luxury hospitality, fast food, insurance, beauty and cosmetics.
Prior to joining IDEO, Wenting was a teaching assistant at the Harvard Graduate School of Design while completing her master's degree in Design Technology. She also worked as a User Experience Designer at Microsoft and Lenovo, with a focus on the development of mobile applications.
