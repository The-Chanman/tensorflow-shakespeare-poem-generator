Michael Chang
Michael Chang is a Principal Business Designer at IDEO who is excited by the potential for technology to improve lives, both directly through personal interaction and by creating new economic opportunities.
Michael has experience across diverse industries such as aerospace & defense, consumer packaged goods, education, healthcare, media & entertainment, retail, technology, and transportation.
Michael has a BS from the California Institute of Technology and an MBA from the Harvard Business School, where he worked on multiple startup projects, serving in strategy, operations, and software development roles.
