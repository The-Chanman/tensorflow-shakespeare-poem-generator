Daniel Marcos Perujo
Daniel is an interaction designer at IDEO Tokyo. He is passionate about designing and developing digital products and experiences for Internet of Things (IoT), with a special focus on mobile technologies. As a designer, he strives to create clarity, efficiency, and opportunities for teaching, across all his work with Japanese clients and agencies over the past 10 years.
Daniel holds a BA degree in graphic design from Granada University in Spain. A fluent Japanese speaker, he's excited about future and clean technologies, especially how they're applied in Japan and around the world.
