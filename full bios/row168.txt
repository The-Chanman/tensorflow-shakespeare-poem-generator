Garrett Winther
A build-to-think Mechanical Engineer at IDEO Chicago, Garrett Winther’s passion for rapid and iterative prototyping enables him to evolve new ideas into tangible concepts in record time. His deep empathy for multi-disciplinary thinking means has allowed him to leverage his engineering knowledge into a diverse portfolio of work ranging from medical devices and packaging designs to digital interactions and retail experiences.
Before joining IDEO, Garrett received his bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology where he lead research in design methodology for MIT's Ideation Lab and lectured for the course "Designing for People.” When not working until the wee hours on IDEO projects, you can find Garrett building toys, developing mobile apps, and poking around the edges of new technologies.
