Sayuri Lovelock
Sayuri is a senior design researcher at IDEO Tokyo. Coming from a mixed race background, she has always shown curiosity in people and how different cultural contexts inform mindsets, behaviors and customs. She is particularly passionate about going deep into analysis, unearthing cultural insights unique to Japan to inform and inspire clients and teams. Prior to IDEO, Sayuri spent 7 years at Flamingo, working on a wide range of categories from beauty, baby products, fashion to cruise ships.
Sayuri has a BA in Social Anthropology and Geography from University College London and hails from the Kansai region of Japan.
