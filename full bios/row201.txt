Jack Haslehurst
Jack is a design and project lead at IDEO Tokyo. With a background across traditional graphic and digital design, film and art direction Jack's role is to make ideas tangible whilst communicating them back to clients and beyond. Since joining IDEO in 2012 he has worked for a broad range of clients across publishing, luxury hospitality, mobility and government services.
A graduate of Chelsea College of Art and Design with first class honours, Jack's interested in helping brands deliver their purpose to employees and customers. Constantly adding to his toolkit, Jack enjoys pushing and experimenting with his own design skills and the design process, experimenting in filming making and 3D digital art, helping to push teams and their work with clients.
