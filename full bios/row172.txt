George Hastings
George Hastings is a multidisciplinary Senior Interaction Designer at IDEO NY. He is passionate about creating smart and effective digital experiences which expertly balance business and user needs.
After getting his degree in business and art from the University of Richmond, George attended the digital design school Hyper Island in Sweden where he studied interactive design. He then dove into the field of UX, working for 3 years at AREA17 where he helped design and ship dozens of websites and apps.
George highly values mastery of his craft. He is equally obsessed with pixel perfect visual design as he is with clean code. From vision, concept and strategy - down to the finest details, George is empathetic, rigorous, and always questioning.
George spends his free time experimenting with machine learning, building fun side projects, and getting inspired.
