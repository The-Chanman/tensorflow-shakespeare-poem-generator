Adam Skaates
Adam Skaates is a Design Director and business lead at IDEO. Adam's a toy inventor at heart, leading a team of designers and engineers, aka inventors, in designing new toys and inventing new ways to play. Adam is passionate about games, toys, apps and technology. His days involve lots of brainstorms, building quick prototypes, telling “toy stories” through videos, and talking with our toy company clients about our inventions and what toys we can invent for them. We geek out about toys, model-making, and the creative process all day long. He has an masters in engineering from Stanford with a focus in Smart Product Design.
