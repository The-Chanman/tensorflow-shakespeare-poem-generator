Anna Stewart
Anna Stewart is a Talent Specialist at IDEO's Chicago studio, where she is delighted to be focusing on HR and Learning & Development. Every day she dives into the opportunity to be optimistic, embrace ambiguity, and make others successful while supporting a dynamic design community.
Prior to IDEO, Anna worked in the event and restaurant industries, where she honed the ability to maintain grace under pressure while managing multiple timelines and varying states of chaos.
Anna graduated from Franklin and Marshall College with a degree in Government and Economics. She loves to dance, enjoys children's books, and attempts to speak French whenever possible.
