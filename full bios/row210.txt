Jarad Bell
Former college ball player turned Designer, Jarad is passionate about tapping into the underlying behaviors, motivations and pain points that drive people's interactions within their world.
As an Interaction Designer and Researcher he leverages his background in Psychology, Human Factors, and Human-Computer Interaction to explore complex problems while focusing on the human. He is passionate about intersection of science, technology and design. Prior to joining IDEO, Jarad worked at Google, AT&T Foundry, and Outlaw Ink. In addition to professional experience, Jarad holds a B.S. in Psychology and a M.S. in Human Factors Engineering, emphasis in Human-Computer Interaction.
