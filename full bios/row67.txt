Bonnie Gartland
Bonnie is the Senior Facilities Specialist at IDEO NY. She designs every aspect of the employee and client experience - - from the softness of the seats, to the scent, and temperature of the air, the details matter where experience is concerned. While Bonnie's focus is largely on physical space, she collaborates closely with experience, business development, and location leads to ensure smooth daily operation.
Bonnie comes to IDEO most recently from General Assembly where she led facilities on a global scale, setting standards and protocol for over 13 tech education campus' around the world.
