Sam Schulman
Sam leads business development at IDEO New York, working with clients and internal design teams to set projects up for market success. 
Sam brings optimism, curiosity and empathy into conversations with clients at IDEO, and helps translate challenges into compelling design opportunities. She focuses specifically on driving change at the individual, collective, and organizational scale across both the public and private sector. Specifically, Sam is interested in the growing intersection of both sectors in her work.
Prior to joining IDEO, Sam spent four years as a Brand Strategist at Interbrand, designing brands and brand experiences, as well as helping client teams to build marketing and brand management capabilities.  
Sam has a Bachelor of Fine Arts from the University of Michigan with a focus in Communication Design.
