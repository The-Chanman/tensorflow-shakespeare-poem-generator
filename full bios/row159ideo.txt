Silvia Vergani
As a Director at IDEO with more than 15 years of experience in research, Silvia guides key clients and teams to uncover people's needs and translate research findings into the design of human-centered products, services, and systems.
Cross-validating insights between ethnographic and data-driven methods, Silvia helps to inform design and business strategies across industries, including health, technology, and transportation. Her passion is leveraging design to impact beliefs and behaviors around horizon topics, such as exploring the future of mental health, aging, and end of life, among others. Silvia also partners with startups and Fortune 500 companies to realize impactful new products and experiences in emerging fields, from neuroscience to AI.
Recent projects include designing a new venture to shape the future of genetics, creating digital interventions for patients with schizophrenia, and envisioning brain-activated homes for injured veterans.
Prior to IDEO, Silvia worked at two MIT startup companies and at management consulting firms, and was a researcher at the University of Venice, Italy.
