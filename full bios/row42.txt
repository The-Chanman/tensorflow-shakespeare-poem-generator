Anthony Stimola
Anthony is s a Communications Designer in the San Francisco based Design for Change studio. His training is in graphic design. He previously worked at SYPartners, working both in the San Francisco and New York office, pushing to bring more digital solutions to projects. Prior to that Anthony worked as a designer/art director at Moving Brands, Hybrid Design, DOT429, and Indicate Design. Anthony received his MFA at the Academy of Art. As a designer Anthony has a deep love for type and swiss design, finding a great amount of inspiration in the realm of social change.
