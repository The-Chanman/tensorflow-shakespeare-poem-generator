Albert Leung
Albert is an engineer and designer who aspires to make great products that can change the world. A design lead at IDEO's Palo Alto studio, he has expertise in a breadth of focus areas, from detailed engineering to strategic product design.
Before joining IDEO, Albert worked at Motorola, Orbit Baby, and Lockheed Martin Space Systems, leveraging complex engineering and human-centered design to launch a string of successful products (including one currently-active spacecraft). Prior to joining the Bay Area studios, Albert worked as a product and interaction designer at IDEO Shanghai. While at IDEO, he has collaborated with startups and established companies to design breakthrough medical devices and consumer electronics.
Albert holds a MS in Product Design from Stanford University and a BS in Mechanical Engineering from Northwestern University. He loves traveling, building vehicles, and expanding his knowledge of culture, science and technology.
