Steve Schwall
Steve Schwall is a Portfolio Director at IDEO Chicago. With a background in mechanical engineering and industrial design, Steve uses his diverse skill set to help clients like Samsung, Ford, Steelcase, Walgreens, and Lilly deliver compelling digital and physical user experiences. Recently, he has developed scalable physical-digital interaction platforms for global corporations while helping local start-ups adopt human-centered design methods.
Prior to IDEO, Steve played with little red wagons as a product design engineer at Radio Flyer. Steve holds a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering and a B.A. in Industrial Design from the University of Notre Dame. In his free time he is a cyclist, skier, juggler, and percussionist.
