Kosta Frantzis
Kosta is interested in the overlap between physical and digital and how the combination of the two can create meaningful and delightful user experiences.
With a background in Electronic engineering and his passion for interaction design he is able to contribute both from a creative and a technical perspective and provide valuable solutions to our clients.
At IDEO he has worked in a variety of industries, ranging from healthcare, to automotive and retail. Prior to IDEO he was involved in the founding phase of two start ups one in healthcare and the other one in education.
He loves bringing ideas to life swiftly and is a firm follower of the build-test-repeat mentality.
