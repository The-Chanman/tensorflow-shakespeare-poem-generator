Jeremy Innes-Hopkins
Jeremy Innes-Hopkins is a Design Lead and Product & Experience designer at IDEO London, where he helps businesses around the world innovate and grow. With a background in Product Design, Jeremy now designs holistic brand experiences working at the intersection of product, digital, service and spatial design. He has a passion for building out experiences, quickly iterating and getting products and services to market at speed. For the past 2 years Jeremy has spent much of his time designing hotels across the globe in America, Europe and Asia, every detail from the overall vision, the staff script to room design and food & beverage.
Jeremy spends much of his spare time on a plane exploring the world, looking for new experiences, meeting new designers and getting out of his comfort zone.
Clients at IDEO include Intercontinental Hotels, British Airways, IKEA and The Empire State building
