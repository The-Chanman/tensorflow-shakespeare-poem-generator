Sloane Beaver
Sloane is a Senior Design Researcher, Brand Strategist, and Project Lead at IDEO in New York. With a background in brand strategy, Sloane has worked across the private and public sector to create differentiated, human-centered products and experiences.
Prior to IDEO, Sloane worked as a Brand Strategist at MullenLowe and The Mom Complex on global consumer brands such as JetBlue, True Religion, and Walmart.
Sloane received a B.A. in English Literature from the University of Virginia, and later completed a Masters of Science in Communications Strategy at the Brandcenter at Virginia Commonwealth University.
