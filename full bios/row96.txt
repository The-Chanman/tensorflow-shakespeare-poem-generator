Christine Hendrickson
Christine Hendrickson works at the global design company IDEO as a Marketing Director on the Global Marketing Communications team. She recently graduated from the Stanford Graduate School of Business and prior to that was IDEO's Bay Area Marketing Director. She held roles in global marketing, public relations and corporate communications after joining the company in 2003 as a recruiter.
While working in the UK for over two years, Christine led marketing for the London location and helped launch an online-innovation platform called OpenIDEO. In 2014, Christine brought IDEO’s 10-year strategic plan to life through a live global event for the organization. She also designed the marketing structure in the Bay Area and led communications for the region. Christine’s work includes exhibiting design thinking on a national and international scale through conferences such as TEDActive and WIRED. Her passions include running, fashion and photography.
