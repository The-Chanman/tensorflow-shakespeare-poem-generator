Peter Bronk
Oct '00

Peter Bronk is a model maker since 1982 experienced in all phases of model making. He has worked in the product design industry way before the innovation of computer aided machining technology and has adapted his skills well to the changing world of prototyping. Peter has many years of experience producing appearance models, prototypes, Engineering check models and mold design/casting. The various techniques he uses range from CAD and CNC technology through to conventional hand working and woodworking skills.
