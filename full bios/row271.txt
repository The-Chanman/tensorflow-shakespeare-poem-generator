Kei Iwashita
Kei is a business designer at IDEO Tokyo, where she focuses on helping clients create human-centered, innovative business models that unlock new ways for companies to grow. She previously worked at IDEO's Food Studio in San Francisco, helping design new products and services that revolutionize the global food landscape. Prior to IDEO, Kei was a consultant at Bain & Company where she worked on strategy, new business development, supply chain management and M&amp;A across various industries ranging from consumer products to private equity. She also joined Spotify Japan as employee #4 to launch the service in Japan.
Kei holds an MBA from the Stanford Graduate School of Business and a B.S. in Organic Chemistry from Brown University
