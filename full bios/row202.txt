Jackie Cooksey
Jacqueline Cooksey is an award winner designer with an entrepreneurial spirit. She draws on nine years experience from a range of disciplines — systems design, strategy, communications design, research, and innovation. She has a passion for field work, disrupting the status quo, being people-centric, and problem solving solutions to have a real, social impact. She works in both public and private sectors with a focus on vulnerable communities in the areas of education, healthcare, financial and public services. Jacqueline holds a bachelor's degree (hons) in communication design from RMIT University in Melbourne, Australia and was a pioneer of a master's in transdisciplinary design from Parsons the New School for Design in New York.
