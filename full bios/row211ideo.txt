Winnie Kwan
Winnie Kwan is a Talent Lead at IDEO New York, where she supports recruiting, learning + development, and HR operations for the studio. Prior to joining IDEO, Winnie oversaw human resources at Harlem Village Academies, where she designed people operations systems to support a network of five charter schools. In another previous life, Winnie managed arts education residency programs, launched publicity campaigns for independent films, and supported media relations for an alternative rock radio station. She holds a BA in English from Brown University and a Master's in Industrial and Labor Relations from Cornell University.
