Benjamin Syverson
Ben Syverson is a Software Design Lead at IDEO, with 25 years of development experience.
Before coming to IDEO, Ben designed, developed and launched many commercial software products for the photography and video markets. At IDEO, Ben has taken his hybrid approach to design and development, and applied it primarily to the challenges of the Health, Wellness and Disease Management space. Whether he's in the operating room talking to doctors, in the studio creating VR prototypes, or leading teams as they design connected systems, Ben has a passion for translating real human needs into powerful impact in the world.
Outside of office hours, Ben continues to design typefaces, manufacture analog cameras, and experiment with new ventures.
