Deirdre Cerminaro
Deirdre Cerminaro is a systems designer in the Design for Learning studio at IDEO. Her background and interests sit at the intersection of strategy, design, behavior, and social innovation.
Prior to IDEO, Deirdre received an MBA at the Yale School of Management. She co-founded the MBA Innovation Summit, an annual event that brings together future business leaders with innovation experts. She was a Junior Architect and Project Manager at Robert A.M. Stern Architects, where she contributed to the design of museums and libraries. She holds a B.A. from Yale University in Cognitive Science.
Outside of work, Deirdre spends as much time as possible outdoors. She is an avid trail runner and cyclist (and has ridden her bike across the country twice).
