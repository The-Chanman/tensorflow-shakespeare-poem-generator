Kirin Swanson
Since joining IDEO in 2011, Kirin has worn a variety of hats that focus on talent within IDEO, including learning & development, mentorship, recruiting & onboarding new employees, and project staffing. She is currently a Talent Lead in the Chicago studio focused on recruiting.
Prior to IDEO, Kirin worked for Net Impact, a network of students and professionals dedicated to driving social and environmental change through business.
Her passion for organizational design has led her to pursue a Masters in Learning and Organizational Change from Northwestern. She also holds a BA in Political Science from the University of Wisconsin.
