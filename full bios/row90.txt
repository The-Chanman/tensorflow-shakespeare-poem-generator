Chris Gold
An engineer’s engineer, Chris Gold hasn’t met a mechanical system he doesn’t like (or like to break apart and rebuild). Chris is passionate about all things technology and is interested in using his skills to influence positive change. He brings to IDEO over 7 years of experience with Xerox shepherding product designs from concept to production. He holds 48 patents in the area of printers and ink stick design. Since joining IDEO in 2011, he has contributed to the design of an innovative drug-delivery system for a major American pharmaceutical company.
Chris earned his bachelor’s and master’s degrees in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Michigan. He’s currently working on becoming both a master woodworker and homebrewer (but not at the same time).
