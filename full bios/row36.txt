Anna Varnai
Anna is a design researcher with IDEO's Munich studio. With her background in psychology and design she is passionate about human-centered and sustainable solutions, that improve people's lives and foster societal change. With a balanced mixture of empathy and creativity she acts as the users' advocate and puts their needs, motivations and behaviours at the core of any innovation project.
Prior to IDEO, Anna was conducting Design Research in the Ghanaian Healthcare Education Sector and worked for the Munich based Hans Sauer Foundation. There she was mainly involved in developing a digital platform for sustainable entrepreneurs. She holds a bachelor's degree in Psychology and a Master's Degree in Design (Technical University Munich). In her master's thesis she focused on human-centered design in the public sector and on how to make services of the foreigner's office (administrative district Munich) more human and effective.
