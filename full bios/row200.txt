Isabela Dias De Mello Sa
Isabela leads design and development of edtech platforms at IDEO. She orchestrates teams of designers, researchers, and developers to apply design and technology to reinvent learning. She has built up a Swiss Army knife of design skills that includes deep experience in Design and Brand Strategy, Research, and User Experience Design.
She has developed apps for teachers to personalize learning in classrooms, engage parents in their child's education through text messaging, and teach literacy to children through an adaptive game platform. Over the years, she has worked with a range of clients inside and outside the education sector from global foundations to local non-profits, tech start-ups to Fortune 500 companies.
Isabela holds a Masters in Design Research from ASU and a Bachelors in Advertising from Potifícia Universidade Católica.
