Misa Misono
Misa is a Design Director in IDEO San Francisco, where she leads the business design discipline and brings over a decade of brand building and insights expertise to projects in retail, food, and technology. She helps teams design breakthrough brands, services, and ventures that unite what users want and what companies need to grow.
Misa previously worked in brand management at Procter and Gamble. A digital leader within the company, she helped Secret Deodorant achieve record-breaking business results and garner national media attention and awards. Prior to P&G, Misa ran her own fashion design business and worked in higher education administration.
She holds an MBA from Duke University and bachelor's degrees in Economics and Political Science from Boston University.
