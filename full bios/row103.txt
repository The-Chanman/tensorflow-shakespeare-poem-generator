Dali Yu
Dali Yu is a Product Designer at IDEO's Palo Alto studio. He blends the creative and technical to develop solutions that don't stay on paper. With a background including Industrial design, communication design, service design and prototyping, he is able to quickly test, iterate and execute various solutions in any medium - physical or digital. Dali has worked with a broad range of clients that range from World Wildlife Fund (designing conservation equipment), to Eli Lilly (designing medical devices), to HTC (designing future product lines).
Dali holds a Bachelor of Science with distinction in Product Design from ArtCenter College of Design in Pasadena, California. Growing up down under in New Zealand where sheep outnumber people six to one, he had a knack for building wooden furniture and life-threatening contraptions.
