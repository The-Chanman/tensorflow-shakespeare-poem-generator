Jayne Jeffries
Jayne Jeffries, PhD, is a Design Researcher with a deep passion for understanding human behavior and building solutions to improve lives. Combining real world experiences and educational degrees from Indiana University, Johns Hopkins, and most recently, a doctorate in social and behavioral sciences from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, she is an interdisciplinary thinker who contributes creative ideas for new research, products, and services. Her expertise combines qualitative methods and advanced statistical analysis, enabling her to use qualitative insights to bring quantitative data to life.
