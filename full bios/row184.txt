Hang IO
Hang is a Communication Designer at IDEO Shanghai and has a passion for creating engaging visual narratives that breathe soul into design. She finds inspiration in human stories, strives for meaningful impact, and enjoys putting a smile on people's faces through her craft.
Prior to joining IDEO, Hang worked as a designer in a global branding agency developing brand identity, visual systems and packaging for clients across a range of industries including FMCG, hospitality, F&B, property development, and tech. Brands she has serviced include Kimberly-Clark, Procter & Gamble, Equatorial Hotel, 1-Net, Selangor Properties, ABC President and Egide.
Hang was born in Macau and worked in Melbourne and Singapore before arriving in Shanghai. She speaks Cantonese, English and Mandarin. Hang holds a M.A. in Communication Design from the Swinburne University of Technology and a B.A. in Communication Design from Monash University.
