Matt Adams
As a Director of IDEO's Product Portfolio, Matt focuses on bringing cutting-edge products to the world in a way that fuses great hardware, software, and human-centered design. Since joining IDEO in 2000, Matt has led dozens of IDEO's most rigorous product engineering projects across the digital and physical landscape, and he brings deep expertise in guiding teams to go from product intention to research, design principles, concepts, and full user interface and industrial design. Matt's work has ranged to include medical devices, consumer electronics, election technology, and underwater robotics.
Matt holds a Bachelor's degree in Manufacturing Engineering from Brigham Young University.
