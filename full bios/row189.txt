Heather Currier
Heather is IDEO's Global Director of Learning & Development and a member of IDEO's Global Talent Team whose areas of focus include: Compensation, Benefits & Policy, Recruiting, Culture and Communications and specifically leads Learning & Development. Working across these talent disciplines has informed a holistic approach she brings to bear when designing the programs that best support a community of creative experts and lifelong learners in multiple disciplines. Whether it's a global onboarding experience or the support of teams across a project's span, Currier Hunt designs for moments of transition to ensure our people are met with what they will need to be the most successful individually and as members of high functioning teams.
Passion areas include nurturing purpose, Diversity and Inclusion, supporting skills for having difficult conversations, Feedback and developing relationships with external partners in Leadership Development and Startups in the talent space.
