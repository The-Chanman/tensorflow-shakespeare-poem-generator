Leesa Gallaher
Leesa is a Communications Designer (writing/strategy) in San Francisco's Hatch studio.
She is passionate about creating beautiful, simple brand experiences - from product ideation, to service design and UX writing.
Prior to joining IDEO, Leesa worked for several design and digital agencies in Sydney, including Interbrand, M&C Saatchi and JWT. Before that, she wrote for pop culture title Oyster Magazine and, in a past life, spent four years working for a boutique social research agency where she got to do her favorite thing: ask why.
Her design strategy work has been recognized and published by D&AD, Graphis Design Annual, Sydney Design Awards and the Australian Graphic Design Association.
