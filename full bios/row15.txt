Alice Kogan
Alice is a Senior Business Designer in IDEO's New York studio. She enjoys integrating data-driven, structured thinking with a focus on human behavior and impactful storytelling. 
Alice's background includes consulting, strategy, finance, and venture capital roles for companies ranging from early stage start-ups to mature multinational firms, consumer facing and B2B, and across the media, technology, health and wellness, and non-profit sectors. She brings experience evaluating complex industries; creating growth, marketing, and pricing strategies; and building financial and operating models for new opportunities. 
Outside of work, Alice is passionate about great film and TV, and made a documentary about immigration experience and identity. 
She holds an MBA from Columbia Business School and a BA in Economics and History from Dartmouth College.
