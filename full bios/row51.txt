Ashley Szukalski
Ashley is an interaction designer at IDEO Tokyo. He works with clients to create captivating and emotionally engaging experiences through interaction and visual design. Prior to IDEO, Ashley worked for the digital agency Critical Mass, designing immersive interactive experiences for numerous international clients. He also worked at Landor Associates, designing for international clients in the fields of branding and packaging design.
Ashley holds a degree in Graphic Design from Ravensbourne in London and during his free time enjoys photography, playing guitar, cycling, as well as experimenting and developing his design skills on personal projects.
