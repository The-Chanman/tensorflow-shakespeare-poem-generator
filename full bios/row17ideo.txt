Lixian Hantover
As Associate Counsel, Lixian serves as a bridge between IDEO's designers and business teams and the international and diverse group of IDEO clients.
Prior to joining IDEO, Lixian worked as a Technology Transaction Associate at Wilson Sonsini Goodrich and Rosati with a focus on representing early stage start-ups.
Before graduating from UCLA School of Law, Lixian worked in film and media both in the United States and in China (and bartended at a hidden whiskey bar in Beijing). She writes frequently for publications such as the ABA Business Law Journal and the Huffington Post on cybersecurity related issues. Â 
In her spare time, Lixian is an award winning songwriter and musician, a student of traditional folk ballads, and a lifelong film buff.
