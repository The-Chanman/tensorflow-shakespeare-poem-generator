Janko Potezica
Janko is a Senior Design Lead for the Mobility Portfolio at IDEO Palo Alto, passionate about building delightful products and connected mobility services. Bringing expertise in software engineering and human-machine interaction, Janko applies a technical and empathic lens to shaping the vehicles and mobility systems of tomorrow.
Prior to IDEO, Janko led interaction design at BMW and oversaw the series production designs for over 30 car models. His responsibilities included creating and validating designs, driving iterations with development, and communicating designs to top-level management. Janko also led strategic projects tackling challenges in the next generation in-vehicle driver experience for the BMW, BMWi and the MINI brand.
Janko holds a Masters degree in Computer and Software Engineering and a BS in Human-Machine Interaction from the Technical University in Munich.
