KK Kelly
Kristin (KK) is a creative with a knack for uncovering human insights that help brands find and establish their voice. She's a connector who understands and cares deeply about the value of relationships and is a restless instigator for crafting authentic ways to get there. Prior to IDEO, KK worked as a copywriter for advertising agencies across the country and had her own brand strategy and communications business. During that time she worked with companies such as Medium and Airbnb, was a Creative Director for a wine meets design start-up, and collaborated with brands such as Verizon, Gap, Skype, Martha Stewart, Stride Rite Shoes, and Boar's Head. She holds a BS in Advertising from The University of Texas at Austin, and is a graduate of the renowned Texas Creative program.
