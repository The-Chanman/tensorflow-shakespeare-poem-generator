Chris Parkhill
As a business operations director in IDEO's Munich studio, Chris' primary aim is to facilitate an environment of success for the leadership and creative teams. He achieves this by focusing on strategic initiatives ranging from process improvement to business development and developing IDEO's external network within the local market. Chris is especially attentive to how IDEO engages with its clients, and is dedicated to working with companies with whom there is a shared purpose and an enthusiastic willingness to achieve common goals.
Before joining IDEO, Chris worked in financial services for both Siemens and EY in both Europe and the Middle East. He is a qualified Chartered Accountant and holds a bachelor's degree in Geography from the University of Leeds.
Having recently found a passion for cycling, Chris spends a good deal of time outside of work training and travelling with his road bike and competing in triathlons.
