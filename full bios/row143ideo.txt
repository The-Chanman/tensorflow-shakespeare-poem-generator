Satoko Tani
Satoko Tani is a business operations lead at IDEO Tokyo, where she designs the business systems and processes that help the studio grow. She's passionate about building the business infrastructure that will help IDEO Tokyo move toward its mission of helping Japan regain its creative confidence. Satoko has worked in a number of startups in industries that span retail, IT, fashion, and advertising. Watching companies grow quickly taught Satoko the importance of building a culture and having a clear sense of mission, learnings that she has brought to her work.
Satoko studied Mechanical Engineering and Oriental Literature at Waseda University in Tokyo.
