Amanda Zacher
Amanda is a Principal Graphic Designer at IDEO. Amanda received her MFA in Graphic Design from the Rhode Island School of Design (RISD) and has worked in architecture, marketing, publishing, and with numerous startups in California, Boston, and New York. Her design experience spans full brand identities, packaging, print (book and magazine), as well as web and mobile interaction design.
Her work has been featured in blogs such as FastCo, Awwwards, and The Best Designs.
