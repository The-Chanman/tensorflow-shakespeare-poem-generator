Katie Clark
Katie Clark is a digital marketing director at IDEO. With a background in branding, illustration, and graphic design, she has worked in the marketing and communication design fields for 15+ years and for IDEO since 2003.
Prior to IDEO, Katie worked for Jacobs Associates and Red Bull, and freelanced for several design studios and publications including The Avocado Group, Wakeboarding Magazine, and WestWayne advertising.
Katie has an MFA from the Academy of Art (graphic design) and a BA in Visual Arts (graphic design and illustration) from Rutgers University.
Outside of IDEO, she is an active health blogger and frequently donates her time and design skills to several nonprofits including the Crohn's and Colitis Foundation of America. Katie is also a classical pianist, guitarist, surfer, and mom.
