Anna Moore Silverstein
Anna Silverstein is Marketing Lead at IDEO New York, where she builds and fosters relationships between IDEO and the vibrant communities of New York City. In this role, Anna designs and produces innovative live events, curates speaker series, engages IDEO designers in lecture, workshop, and teaching opportunities worldwide, and creates platforms for sharing IDEO's work in media, finance, retail, hospitality, and the public sector.
Anna draws upon her decade-long experience as an actor, improviser, and theater producer to nurture meaningful and productive connections between people. In her work, Anna affirms the overlap between improv and design thinking by championing the foundational importance of listening, acceptance, and discovery in both disciplines. She holds a BA in English from Cornell University and an MFA in Acting from American Conservatory Theater.
