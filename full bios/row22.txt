Amelia Juhl
Amelia is a design researcher at IDEO Tokyo. Raised in a multicultural community in Tokyo, she has engaged with many different cultures and perspectives. In her role, she enjoys unlocking insights that lead to opportunities for design. Amelia brings more than 15 years of experience leading creative projects of all sizes, building and managing client relationships, and moves comfortably between strategic planning and execution. Prior to IDEO, Amelia was associate planning director at Wunderman, working with multiple consumer brands including Nissan, Microsoft, and P&G.
Amelia graduated from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor with a degree in International Relations.
