Sven Stumm
As an interaction designer at IDEO Munich, Sven creates innovative design solutions with a meaningful and human-centered approach, while being especially interested in the intersections between digital and physical experiences. He enjoys doing a little bit of everything, from visual and interaction design to prototyping, programming and motion.
Sven holds a Bachelor's degree in Interaction Design from HfG Schwäbisch Gmünd and spent one semester studying Media Design at Victoria University of Wellington, New Zealand. During his studies, he worked as an intern at AllofUs, London, and at Artefact in Seattle.
