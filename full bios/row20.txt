Amanda Briden
Amanda leads the consulting team at OI Engine, IDEO's open innovation SaaS business. OI Engine offers a platform and design thinking consulting services to help organisations establish an innovation process that is open to all, creative, transparent, positive and highly collaborative – a place where employees achieve more, together. OI Engine powers the award winning OpenIDEO and Teacher's Guild. 
Prior to this, she worked in social innovation at a UK based social enterprise, called Participle. Participle designs and then scales new UK public services and Amanda was in charge of measuring the social impact of these new services.
Before completing her MBA at the University of Oxford, she was an aerospace engineer at NASA and was responsible for training multidisciplinary teams of engineers, designing mission operations and assessing risk.
