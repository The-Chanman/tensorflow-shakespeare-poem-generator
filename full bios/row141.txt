Ed White
Ed White is senior editor at IDEO Europe. Ed was formerly editor of Contagious magazine, a quarterly B2B print magazine and website for marketers dedicated to marketing innovation, consumer behaviour and emerging technology. He was also a senior consultant at Contagious' digital innovation consultancy Insider, working with clients including Nike, BBC, LVMH, Google, and Diageo. As a freelance journalist, Ed has written for magazines including Wired, Viewpoint, and Protein.
