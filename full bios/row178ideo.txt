Tejpal Dhillon
Tej Dhillon is a Business Designer in IDEO's Chicago studio. He's passionate about using business thinking to inspire and focus design. He's specially interested in bringing new products/services to market through strategic thinking and prototyping businesses.
At IDEO, Tej has worked across a wide range of industries including financial services, transportation, utilities, retail, CPG, food, and consumer technology. Across these industries Tej provided product and business model innovation, strategic advisory, service design, brand development and business experimentation.
Prior to IDEO, Tej worked for Ernst and Young in their financial services advisory group and participated in the Clinton Fellowship for Service in India.
Tej holds an MBA from the Kellogg School of Management at Northwestern University and a BS from University of Rochester in Mechanical Engineering. In his free time Tej loves to practice martial arts, DJ and read mystic philosophy.
