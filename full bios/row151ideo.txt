Sebastien Park
Sebastien Park is a Senior Business Designer with a background in design-driven strategy and venture building. He brings experience working with high-growth consumer companies tackling systemic challenges across categories like food, mobility, financial services, and media.
The scope of his work centers around helping founders swiftly explore/identify opportunities; guiding teams towards evaluative prototypes that inform + inspire; and in advising organizations on entering new markets with successful offerings.
He was previously an associate at New York-based venture capital firm Collaborative Fund; supporting creative entrepreneurs pushing the world forward, and an early investor in companies like Kickstarter, Lyft, and Reddit. In his spare time, he mentors at NEW INC, serves as a trustee for the Awesome Foundation, and sits on the advisory board for the CFDA Launch Pad.
