Shelagh McLellan
Shelagh is a principal interaction designer with a focus on translating user research insights to interfaces, services and systems. She is intrigued by human behavior and has a desire to create user experiences that impact people’s lives positively and resonate emotionally. Shelagh has worked on visual and interactive interfaces, for both digital platforms and physical products. Shelagh has worked with a range of different industries covering from healthcare, information technology, industry machinery and lifestyle products.
Prior to IDEO, Shelagh has worked at Facebook, the Danish design consultancy design-people, was the principal designer for a healthcare startup and has designed shoes and accessories for John Fluevog Shoes.
Shelagh holds an MFA in Interaction Design from Umeå Institute of Design in Sweden and a B.Des in Industrial Design from Emily Carr University of Art + Design, in Vancouver, Canada.
