Akshan Ish
Akshan is a Digital Design Researcher at IDEO Munich, where his primary focus is to understand how technology influences people's behaviours. He inquires into what people's digital lives can tell us about the societies we live in today, in order to inspire systems and services that will have an impact on how they might be shaped in the future. In his spare time, he dreams about reviving his rap career.
He holds a Masters in Graphic Design from the National Institute of Design in Ahmedabad, India and a Bachelors Degree in Computer Science from Jaypee Institute of Information Technology in Noida, India. Prior to IDEO, Akshan worked as an Information Designer with the Public Health Foundation of India and as a freelance designer on various civic interest design projects.
