Elyssa He
As a translator in IDEO's Shanghai office, Elyssa He provides consecutive English/Chinese translation. Her expertise in language and cultural interpretation helps to bring insights to her project teams while facilitating efficient and easy communication. Elyssa joined IDEO as translator for project teams conducting field research and workshops with clients in China.
Originally from Shanghai, Elyssa He completed her Master's Degree in Translation and Interpretation from Shanghai International Studies University.
