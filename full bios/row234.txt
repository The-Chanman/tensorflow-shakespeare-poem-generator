John Robichau
John Robichau is a Project Coordinator in IDEO's New York studio. He develops relationships between IDEO NY and the clients we serve and is dedicated to helping teams navigate the complexities of project development with ease and excitement.
John holds a Bachelor of Fine Arts from NYU’s Tisch School of the Arts. He’s worked widely in the performing arts, supporting the development of training programs and full-scale productions in NYC and abroad.
Most recently, John made chocolate in Brooklyn with the Mast Brothers, where his focus was firmly on craft manufacturing and community development. He played a central role maintaining those values while growing the business through the development of new wholesale partnerships.
In his time away from IDEO, he continues to perform, study, and teach various theater forms such as acting, puppetry, mask, stilt-walking, and live stunt performance.
