Lindsey Armeen
Lindsey Armeen is an Executive Support Lead who artfully plays air-traffic-control at the intersection of IDEO's senior leadership, clients, and vibrant design community. Since joining IDEO in 2006, she's made it her mission to enable leading-edge creative work by handling the details for project teams, supporting client relationships, and amplifying the impact of IDEO's senior-most leaders.
Prior to IDEO, Lindsey worked in the finance and restaurant industries, from which she gained an an eye for numbers, appreciation for extraordinary customer service, and passion for food culture.
Lindsey graduated from the University of San Diego where she majored in Urban Studies and Spanish Literature. A year abroad in France and Italy began a life of embracing adventure and all opportunities to practice her language skills.
