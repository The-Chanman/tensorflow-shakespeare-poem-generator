Yuni Lee
Yuni Lee is a Design Lead at IDEO San Francisco, where she guides teams with diverse skillsets to create physical and digital experiences. With expertise in service design, interaction design, and strategy, she is able to create holistic solutions for clients across the technology, ﬁnancial, and retail industries. 
Prior to joining IDEO, Yuni was a user experience designer at BBC in London, where she developed and launched world leading services on mobile platforms for the Sports, News and iPlayer departments.
Yuni holds a degree in Industrial Design from Brunel University in West London where she presented  her final project to Her Majesty the Queen and HRH The Duke of Edinburgh.
Having lived in Korea, England, and now the U.S., Yuni has a global perspective that informs the way she thinks about culture and product design.
