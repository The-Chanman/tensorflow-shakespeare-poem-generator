Momo Miyazaki
Momo Miyazaki is an interaction designer at IDEO. She is inspired by the curious web of relationships that make up people's worlds. She has designed intuitive digital and physical experiences in the forms of healthcare platforms, automotive experiences, financial products, and educational tools. Her personal work has been exhibited at Miraikan (The National Museum of Emerging Science and Innovation, Japan) and the Swiss Science Center Technorama, short-listed for IXDA Interaction Awards, and featured in The Economist, DVice, Creative Applications, and Gizmodo.
Momo received a Masters from the Copenhagen Institute of Interaction Design. She worked as a buyer for the Museum of Modern Art and a watchmaker at dedegumo after receiving her BS in Fine Art (metalsmithing) from Skidmore College.
