Martin Kay
Martin Kay is a portfolio director at IDEO's San Francisco studio, leading creative teams and helping to guide the industrial design discipline. Whether he's collaborating with global corporations or disruptive startups, his focus is on holistic design, connecting products, interactions, space, and brand identity.
Martin has more than a decade of design consulting experience across a broad range of industries, including electronics, soft goods, food and beverage, transportation, beauty, and hospitality. His work has received IDEA and RSA awards and been featured in exhibitions. His prior experience includes working at the London design firm PDD Group. He holds a bachelor's degree in industrial design from the University of Salford, Manchester.
Martin is originally from Manchester, England; in his free time he likes to paint, cook, and mix a mean cocktail.
