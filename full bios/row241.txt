Josh Finkle
Josh Finkle is a Principal Industrial Designer with a passion for toy invention and bringing ideas to life through sketching and prototyping! Prior to joining IDEO, he designed toys at Little Tikes, Radio Flyer, and Automoblox, taking ideas from concept, through manufacturing, to market. Josh studied Industrial Design at Carnegie Mellon University where he was part of a team building robots for the Human Computer Interaction Institute. Josh believes everyone is inherently creative and helps teach courses in Product Design at Stanford University. Outside of design Josh is into woodworking, cycling, and running marathons.
