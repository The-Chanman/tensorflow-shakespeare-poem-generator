Nicholas Pajerski
Nic is a Senior Environments Designer in the Design for Change Studio. He specializes in physical + digital prototyping and helps navigate the tricky terrain of Organizational Design with through tangible experiences. Getting touchy feely with it all.
With home base at IDEO San Francisco, Nic has worked for extended periods in satellite offices in Lima, Peru and Singapore. Concurrently pursuing his passion for academia he teaches Architecture at UC Berkeley, and Interaction Design at the California College of the Arts. He holds a Masters in Architecture from Princeton University.
