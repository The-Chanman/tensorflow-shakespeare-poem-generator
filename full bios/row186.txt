Hannah Williams
Hannah Williams is the Health Portfolio Coordinator at IDEO, Palo Alto. She has a passion for enabling creativity, elevating the human experience and provoking discussion on under-explored topics and perspectives.
With experience in project management for business development, digital animation, UI/UX, web design, and graphic design, Hannah helps keep the trains of the Health Portfolio running on time.
Hannah has a BA from Dartmouth College where she studied Digital Arts with a focus on 3D modeling, and Government as it pertained to foreign policy, leadership and grand strategy.
