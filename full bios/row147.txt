Eri Sugiura
Eri is a Project Coordinator at IDEO Tokyo. She helps project teams and clients achieve success by helping organize and frame design challenges, with a focus on helping teams deliver world class design research. Prior to joining IDEO, Eri worked at Meiji University as an international officer, developing partnerships and programs with institutions around the world.
Eri holds a MSEd in Higher Education from the University of Pennsylvania and a BA in Behavioral Science/Anthropology from Chiba University. Eri is passionate about education, especially on the impact of design thinking in learning processes and education systems.
