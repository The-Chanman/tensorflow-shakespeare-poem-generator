Brian Pelsoh
Brian Pelsoh is a design lead at IDEO San Francisco where he designs experiences, identities, and communications. With over ten years of experience in all facets of human centered design; he specializes in designing with empathy to craft compelling user experiences, brand expressions, and stories. He is a designer that believes in thinking through making, following intuition, and doing work that matters.
Prior to IDEO, Brian worked at the branding firms Pentagram and VSA Partners where he worked with clients large and small to bring form to their identity and communications. He cut his teeth in design leading small teams of designers at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago and the Milwaukee Art Museum.
He holds an MFA in graphic design from the Maryland Institute College of Art and a BFA in communication design from the Milwaukee Institute of Art & Design.
