Joshua Aleta
Joshua Aleta joined IDEO in 2005 as a Project Space Coordinator. Working closely with Site Services, Joshua assists with the placement and setup of project spaces to support the IDEO community.
Joshua has been involved with the coordination and setup of project spaces to provide a comfortable place for each upcoming project to reside. He has also worked on the IDEO archiving system, contributing his skills in organization and data entry.
Prior to IDEO, Joshua worked in retail, where he honed his skills in communication and attention to detail.
Joshua is an avid sports and music fan. His computer skills include the IDEO archiving system, Outlook and Windows.
