CHRIS'S BIOGRAPHY
Christina is the Talent Lead at IDEO Cambridge. She is responsible for both Human Resources and Recruiting for the Cambridge office and also manages the 401(k) plan for all of IDEO.

Christina earned her Bachelor’s Degree in Sociology from Boston University and received her Master’s Degree in Human Resources from Suffolk University.
